About Me

My name is Ryan Damron. I am married to Lisa, and our kids are Annabelle, Hutchison, and Ruth. I have been working in the field of education in some capacity since 2007. I have worked in day cares, summer camps, children's ministry, and public education. I taught elementary school from first grade to fifth grade (not fourth!), and I was even a librarian for 2 years. For the last 2 years I have been teaching pre-school and kindergarten students over Zoom. It has been a blast!

I love music, mostly country. But good country, not the stuff you hear all over the radio. I play guitar, and dabble on banjo. My kids love singing old country songs with me. My daughter Annabelle is learning to play guitar, and my son Hutchison is learning to drum. Yikes!

Lisa stays at home with the kids. She is a virtual bookkeepper and is definitely the brains of the family. We had a house built, and moved into it in March of 2021.
